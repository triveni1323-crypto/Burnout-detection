What is Student Burnout? 🤔

Student burnout is a state of chronic stress that leads to physical and emotional exhaustion, cynicism and detachment, and feelings of ineffectiveness and lack of accomplishment.

Studies show that over 40% of students experience burnout symptoms during their academic career — most without even realizing it until their grades drop significantly.

⚠️ Common Warning Signs:

  • 😴 Constantly feeling tired even after sleeping
  • 📉 Declining academic performance
  • 😔 Loss of motivation to study
  • 🧠 Difficulty concentrating for long periods
  • 😤 Increased irritability and frustration
  • ⌨️ Slower typing and response times
